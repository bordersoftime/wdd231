Membership Levels

Gold Membership

$1000/year

Benefits:

  • Featured placement on the chamber's homepage
  • Dedicated spotlight section in monthly newsletter
  • Priority booth location at chamber events
  • Complimentary entry to all chamber events
  • Access to exclusive networking sessions
  • Full-page advertisement in annual business directory
  • Direct referrals from chamber staff

Silver Membership

$500/year

Benefits:

  • Company listing in chamber's online directory
  • Regular mentions in monthly newsletter
  • Discounted booth rates at chamber events
  • 50% discount on chamber event entry fees
  • Half-page advertisement in annual business directory
  • Business promotion on chamber social media

Bronze Membership

$250/year

Benefits:

  • Basic listing in chamber's online directory
  • Invitation to monthly networking events
  • 25% discount on chamber event entry fees
  • Quarter-page advertisement in annual business directory
  • Access to chamber resources and local business data

Non-Profit Membership

Free

Benefits:

  • Basic listing in chamber's online directory
  • Access to networking events
  • Opportunity to collaborate with local businesses
  • Limited access to chamber resources
  • Eligibility for assistance from chamber volunteers
